Affordable Motel Rooms in Virden, MB

Enjoy comfort and convenience when you stay at Countryside Inn Motel. For more than 30 years, we’ve been a peaceful respite for people traveling to or through Virden. Situated just between Moose Mountain Provincial Park and Spruce Woods Provincial Park, Countryside Inn Motel is Virden’s comfortable and affordable lodging choice.

Our 36 single and double occupancy rooms are perfect for a peaceful night’s sleep in Virden. Each of our smoking and non-smoking rooms features flat-screen TVs equipped with digital cable television, free high-speed wireless internet and comfortable, spacious beds. We also have an outdoor BBQ area, the perfect spot to relax and unwind after a full day of exploring beautiful Virden.
